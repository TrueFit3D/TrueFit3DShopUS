How custom orders work

Simple process. Clear communication. No guesswork.

1) Send details

STL/STEP preferred. Or photos + dimensions + what it needs to do.

2) Choose material

We’ll recommend based on strength, heat, UV, and environment.

3) Quote & timeline

Pricing based on size, time, material, and finishing needs.

4) Print & ship

Quality checked before packaging. Tracking provided on shipment.

FAQ

Clear answers = fewer back-and-forth messages.

Do you do custom design / modeling?

We can help with minor edits and print-ready adjustments. For full modeling, we can discuss scope and pricing.

What file types do you accept?

STL and STEP are best. We can also work with OBJ and 3MF in many cases.

How do you price custom prints?

Pricing is based on material, print time, size, complexity, and any finishing. We’ll provide a quote before printing.

Can I order ready-to-ship items?

Yes — browse our Etsy shop for keychains, gifts, décor, and seasonal items. Custom colors/personalization may be available.

Do you offer bulk or repeat orders?

Yes. If you need multiple units, we can optimize settings and pricing for small-batch runs.
